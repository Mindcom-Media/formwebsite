Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Form ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Camera Data: Form uses your device's camera to perform real-time motion analysis. Video data is processed on-device and is not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience, such as feature usage frequency and session duration.
  • Device Information: Basic device information including device model, operating system version, and app version.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the motion analysis service
  • Improve and optimize the app's performance
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Communicate with you about updates or support

4. Data Storage & Security

Your motion analysis data is processed locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data that is transmitted to our servers. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies, including:

  • Apple App Store (for distribution and payments)
  • Analytics services (anonymized usage data only)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Form is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@formapp.com.
