Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2026

1. Who We Are

Form ("the App") is operated by Mindcom Media Ltd ("we", "us", "our"), a company registered in the United Kingdom.

Contact: Tim O'Shea
Email: hello@getunlocked.ai

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and applicable US state privacy laws, Mindcom Media Ltd is the data controller.

2. Summary (Apple App Store Privacy Disclosure)

In line with Apple's App Store privacy framework, Form is registered as "Data Not Collected". This means:

  • We do not collect any data from the App.
  • We do not link any data to your identity.
  • We do not use any data for tracking purposes as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that collect, transmit, or share user data.

All exercise analysis, scoring, and recommendations are performed entirely on your device. Form does not require an account, does not require an internet connection, and makes no network requests during normal operation.

3. What Data is Processed On-Device

Although we do not collect data, the App necessarily processes information locally on your device to provide its core functionality. This information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export it.

Data processed locally on your device includes:

  • Preferences & Settings: Training focus, experience level, favourite exercises, notification preferences, and capture settings.
  • Exercise Session Data: Exercise type, date, duration, form analysis scores, sub-scores across scoring categories, per-rep analysis, and session notes.
  • Video & Audio Data: Exercise videos you record or import are stored locally in the App's sandboxed document directory. Recorded videos may include audio captured by your device's microphone. These files never leave your device unless you explicitly share or export them.
  • Body Pose Data: Anonymised joint position data (17 body points) extracted from your videos using Apple's on-device Vision framework. This is not biometric identification data — it analyses body movement patterns only, with no facial recognition or identity inference.
  • Drill & Progress Data: Drill completion records and daily focus recommendations generated locally.

We have no access to any of the above data.

4. How Your Data Is Used

All data processing occurs entirely on your device for the following purposes:

  • Analysing your exercise form using Apple's on-device Vision framework
  • Tracking your progress and generating Form Scores
  • Providing personalised daily focus recommendations
  • Sending local notifications for daily reminders (if enabled)
  • Allowing you to export your data at your discretion

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose beyond providing the App's core functionality.

5. Permissions We Request

Form requests the following device permissions. You may grant or revoke any permission at any time via your device's Settings app.

  • Camera (NSCameraUsageDescription): Used solely to record exercise videos for on-device form analysis. Video footage is processed locally and is never transmitted externally.
  • Microphone (NSMicrophoneUsageDescription): When recording video, audio may be captured as part of the standard video file. Audio is stored only on your device and is not analysed or transmitted.
  • Photo Library (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription / PHPickerViewController): Used to let you select videos from your photo library for analysis. The App uses Apple's standard photo picker, which provides access only to the items you select.
  • Notifications (UNUserNotificationCenter): Used to deliver local reminders if you enable them. All notifications are scheduled locally; no push servers are involved.

Form does not request access to Health data, location, contacts, calendars, motion & fitness, or any other sensitive data category.

6. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, our legal basis for processing is:

  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)): Processing your exercise data locally on your device to provide the App's core functionality. Since no data leaves your device, the impact on your privacy is minimal.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): For optional features such as local notifications, camera, microphone, and photo library access, which you can enable or disable at any time via system settings.

Since all processing occurs on-device and no personal data is transmitted to us, server-side GDPR obligations do not apply.

7. Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework and the App's sandboxed document directory. Specifically:

  • Session data, scores, and preferences are stored in a SQLite database within the App sandbox, protected by iOS data protection.
  • Videos are stored as MP4 files in the App's document directory.
  • Frame analysis data is stored as JSON files locally.

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and App sandboxing. We have no access to your data.

8. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share your data with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any external servers.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, attribution, or tracking SDKs are integrated.
  • No crash reporting services that collect personal data are used.
  • No social media SDKs are integrated.

The only ways your data leaves the App are if you explicitly:

  • (a) export your data using the "Export All Data" feature in Settings, or
  • (b) share a video or analysis result through the iOS system share sheet.

In both cases, you control the destination, and we receive nothing.

9. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Form does not engage in tracking as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. We do not access the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), and we do not link any data collected within the App to data collected in other apps or websites for advertising or measurement purposes. The App therefore does not present an ATT prompt.

10. Accounts and In-App Purchases

Form does not require an account or sign-in. There is no registration, no username, no password, and no cloud profile associated with your use of the App. Because no account exists, there is no account to delete — uninstalling the App permanently removes all associated data from your device.

Form does not currently offer in-app purchases or subscriptions.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

Under GDPR / UK GDPR:

  • Right of access: View all your data directly within the App, or export it as JSON / CSV.
  • Right to rectification: Edit session notes within the App.
  • Right to erasure: Delete individual sessions or all data via Settings > Your Data > Delete All Data.
  • Right to data portability: Export all your data as a structured file.
  • Right to restrict processing: Uninstall the App to stop all processing.
  • Right to object: Since processing is local and essential to the App's function, objection can be exercised by discontinuing use.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You may contact the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) or your local supervisory authority.

Under US State Privacy Laws (CCPA/CPRA, Virginia CDPA, Colorado CPA, Connecticut CTDPA, and others):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected.
  • Right to delete personal information.
  • Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information — we do not sell or share your data.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.
  • Right to data portability.

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly within the App without needing to contact us.

12. Children's Privacy

Form is not directed at children. In line with the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. In line with the UK GDPR and EU GDPR, we do not knowingly process data from children under the age of 16 (or the equivalent age set by your member state).

Because no data is transmitted to us, we cannot identify users by age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App and you would like guidance, please contact us at hello@getunlocked.ai.

The App carries an Apple age rating of 4+, reflecting that it contains no objectionable content; however, the fitness guidance and exercises shown are intended for adult use under appropriate supervision.

13. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the App is installed. You may delete individual sessions or all data at any time from within the App via Settings > Your Data. Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all associated data from your device. Because we hold no copy of your data, there is nothing for us to delete on our side.

14. International Data Transfers

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, no international data transfers take place. Your data remains on your device regardless of your location.

15. Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control

Form does not track you across other apps or websites. We honour Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control signals by design, as no tracking occurs whatsoever.

16. Apple Privacy Manifest

The App ships with an Apple Privacy Manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy) that declares its use of required-reason APIs and confirms that no tracking domains are contacted and no data types are collected, in accordance with Apple's App Store requirements.
